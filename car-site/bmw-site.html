ATHLETIC COUPÉ SILHOUETTE

The car, with sculpted lines and a smoothly sloping coupé roof, combined with exclusive lightweight alloy wheels and aerodynamic M exterior mirrors, radiates concentrated sporty energy. The elongated silhouette ends with a striking rear section that embodies restrained power.

LED HEADLIGHTS & TAILLIGHTS

Rear lights, like headlights, use exclusively LED light
functions. The elongated L-shaped design with closed
lighting lines ensures a smooth transition into the trunk
lid and highlights the elegance of the rear body.

M ENGINE COMPETITION

The 3.0-liter M TwinPower Turbo engine combines the best of two worlds: the ability to reach high revolutions — up to 7,600 rpm — and enormous torque of 550 Nm. Direct injection with twin turbocharging and precise tuning guarantee explosive acceleration and confident traction. Numerous innovations deliver unprecedented dynamics, emphasized by the unmistakable and instantly recognizable “voice” of the M engine.
